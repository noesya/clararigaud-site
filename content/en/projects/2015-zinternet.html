The Zinternet project - external link is an interactive drawing experience imagined as part of the development of the Zinefest website - external link in 2015.

year: 2015 date: 2015 contents_reading_time: seconds: 27 text: >- less than a minute contents: - kind: block template: chapter title: >- slug: >- ranks: self: 2 data: layout: no_background text: >-

For my studies, I had to make a website, and I don't like things that do nothing but get a grade. I therefore proposed Disparate - external link, which organizes this micro-edition festival, to make the website of the festival free of charge.

The idea behind Zinternet is a white page on which all visitors to the site can draw. The drawings produced by visitors are also posted on the Tumblr - external link of the project. These drawings are then published as a fanzine available at free price in support of the Zinefest festival.

Today, he continues to accompany the Zinefest festival year after year, welcoming drawings for each new edition.
