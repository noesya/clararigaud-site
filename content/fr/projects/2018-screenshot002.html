Screenshot_002 est la troisième édition du projet Screenshot sortie en 2018

year: 2018 date: 2018 projects_categories: - "random-pixel-order" contents_reading_time: seconds: 25 text: >- moins d'une minute contents: - kind: block template: chapter title: >- slug: >- ranks: self: 2 data: layout: no_background text: >-

Sreenshot_002 featured the work of the following artists: Aaron Juarez - lien externe, Alpha Rats - lien externe, Bart Pilarczyk - lien externe, Boris Detraz - lien externe, Dream Doctor, Paulin Grenade - lien externe, Jay Cartis - lien externe, Kaspar - lien externe, Léon Denise - lien externe, Paul Vivien - lien externe, Althaë - lien externe

For the cover, I decided to work with 3D in blender to create a fleet of jellyfish made up of cathodic screens from which the images escape. The cover was screen printed on a black 220g mat paper

We organized an evening at Nogozon, in Paris, to celebrate the release of the book and to give the various participants and the public an opportunity to meet around concerts and VJing performances.
